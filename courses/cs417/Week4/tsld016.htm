Using a Lamport Time Stamp

  • A Lamport Time Stamp contains two fields:

    • Counter.
    • Process id.

  • When sending a message.

    • Increment your counter.
    • Stamp your message.
    • Send your message.

  • When receiving a message

    • Adopt the counter on the message if it is bigger than your local counter.

  • Unique for every message.

  • Agreed order of messages can be achieved by comparing (counter, process id) on message.

    • Might be useful to add an index that is incremented only when sending new messages (not part of LTS).
