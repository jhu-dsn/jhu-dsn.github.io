    Quick Design Problem!

  • A company’s Web site has 50 servers serving content. The company’s revenue depends on exact logs of how many pages are viewed and by what people.
  • Each server produces about 50 log entries per second. Each log entry is about 200 bytes. All logs need to end up in at least one computer. All 50 servers are in one geographic location. A fast network with several segments connects them.
  • Design a system to achieve this task.
    • GOAL: correctness, low cost, scalability, simplicity, ease of setup, robustness.
    • What if the servers are split between 2 location (East and West Coast)?
    • What if real time monitoring is also needed.
