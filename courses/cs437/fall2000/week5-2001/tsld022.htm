The Protocol (cont.)

    Step 2:
    Broadcast Bid(Iteration,x)
    wait for n-f messages of type Bid(Iteration,*)
    v is the real value (0/1) occurring most often
    and m is the number of occurrences of v
    if m >= n-2f
    then Decide (x=v)
    else if m >= n-4f
    then x = v
    else x = random (0 or 1)
