    System Design Exercise

  • Web based content site has 100 servers serving content. The company’s revenue depends on exact logs of how many pages are viewed and by what people.
  • Each server produces about 500 log entries per second. Each log entry is about 100 bytes. All logs need to end up in at least one location. All 100 servers are in one geographic location. A fast network with several segments connects them.
  • Design a system to achieve this task.
    • GOAL: correctness, low cost, scalability, simplicity, ease of setup.
  • If the servers are in 2 locations (East and West coasts)?
  • If real-time monitoring as well as offline auditing is required?
