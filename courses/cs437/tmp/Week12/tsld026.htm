A Four Nights Protocol

  • If you know of k = 0 (mod 3) cheating husbands, fire on the first night

  • If you know of k = 1 (mod 3) cheating husbands, fire on the second night

  • If you know of k = 2 (mod 3) cheating husbands, fire on the third night

  • If your husband cheats, shoot him on the forth night. (There is at least one cheating husband. So, if shootings occur only on one of the first 3 nights, all of the husbands cheat.)
