The ISIS Toolkit

  • Initiated at Cornell University, 1983.

  • Tightly coupled distributed systems that are developed over loosely coupled processors.

  • Defines how to:

    • create, join and leave a group.
    • map a symbolic group name to a communication handler.
    • send a message and collect acks from all/part of the group.
    • determine the group membership.
