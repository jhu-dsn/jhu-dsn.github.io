Resources for Distributed Operating Systems

A number of good books exist:
  • Mukesh Singhal & Niranjan G. Shivaratri, Advanced Concepts in Operating Systems, McGraw-Hill (1994)
  • R. Chow and T. Johnson, Distributed Operating Systems & Algorithms, Addison-Wesley (1997)
  • G. J. Nutt, Centralized and Distributed Operating Systems, Prentice-Hall (1992)
  • M. Raynal, Algorithms for Mutual Exclusion, MIT Press (1986)
  • M. Raynal, Distributed Algorithms and Protocols, Wiley & Sons (1988)
  • M. Raynal, Networks and Distributed Computation, MIT Press (1988)
  • P. K. Sinha, Distributed Operating Systems: Concepts and Design, IEEE Press (1997)
  • A. S. Tanenbaum, Distributed Operating Systems, Prentice-Hall (1995)
