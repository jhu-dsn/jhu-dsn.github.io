Overlay Networks – Why ?

  • Experimentation and deployment flexibility

    • Excellent research platform

  • Overlay networks provide scalable functionality

    • Service scalability: multiple independent instances
      • IP Multicast failed because it relies on its global deployment
    • Relatively small (1000 nodes)
    • Optimized algorithms based on more information
      • From the network participants
      • From the application

  • Overlays are a powerful concept that extends the use of our networking services
