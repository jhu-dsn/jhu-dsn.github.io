Instructor

Students will review, present, and discuss current research in computer systems, distributed systems, and computer networks, in the contexts of dependability, performance and scalability.

Here are some potential papers to read and discuss during the semester (list will be updated based on class interests):

  • C3: Internet-Scale Control Plane for Video Quality Optimization

  • Existential Consistency: Measuring and Understanding Consistency at Facebook

  • Attacking the Network Time Protocol

  • BGP Security in Partial Deployment

  • Why Is It Taking So Long to Secure Internet Routing?

  • Empirical Development of a Trusted Sensing Base for Power System Infrastructures

  • Matchmaking for Online Games and Other Latency-Sensitive P2P Systems - Paper for 4/14/2016
