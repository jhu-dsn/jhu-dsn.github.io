Why would one use ATM or Frame Relay

  • The ‘One-hop Network’

    • Some people like to see just one hop between two sites when they do a traceroute.
    • These people seem to believe that a path that has one hop in it is somehow better than one that has several. This could not be further from the truth.
      • There are in fact multiple hops in the path, they just are layer 2 hops, instead of layer 3 hops.
