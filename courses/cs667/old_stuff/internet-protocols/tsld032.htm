Multiple egress points

  • Assume that Erols peers with every provider who appears at an exchange that Erols also appears at.

    • The routers will use BGP to first figure out which network (AS) to send a packet to.
    • Once that has been determined, the routers will use OSPF to determine which available exchange is closest.
      • With those two pieces of information, the router now knows what the ‘best’ path is.
