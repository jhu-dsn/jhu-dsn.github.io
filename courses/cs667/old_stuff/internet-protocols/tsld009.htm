Inter-AS Routing (BGP metrics)

  • BGP has many metrics that can be modified appropriately.

    • I will not go through all of them here, but I will talk about the most important ones.
    • The BGP metric that is most often used is the AS path.
      • The AS path accumulates as a route travels from AS to AS via BGP. Every time the route goes from one AS to another one, the AS the route just left is put on the left side of the existing AS path.
