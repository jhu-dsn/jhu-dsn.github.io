How does Internet routing work today?

  • First some terms

    • Autonomous System (AS)
      • An autonomous system is a group of routers that are all under one administrative domain.
        • For example, MCI, Sprint, UU-Net and Erols are all autonomous systems.
        • Each autonomous system has a unique sixteen bit identifier. For the above autonomous systems they are 3561, 1239, 701, and 6079 respectively.
