PPT Slide

    Pre-80’s -Supercomputers were either the vector multi-
    processors paradigm or massively parallel architectures.
    1986 - Remote-Unix project implemented
    - “Seamless web” idea conceived. The idea is to link user
    interface on the workstations and supercomputers.
    1987 - “Metacomputing” is coined by Larry Smarr
    1988 - CM-2 and Cray-2 connected together via LAN,
    demonstrating that architecturally different
    supercomputers can work together to improve
    performance.
    - “Grand Challenges” coined. These are major problems of
    science or society that would push the limits of current
    computing technology. Simulations are one class.
