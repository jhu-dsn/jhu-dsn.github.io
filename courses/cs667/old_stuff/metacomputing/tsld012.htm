PPT Slide

    1991 -Congress passed the The High Performance Computing and Communications Act. The act established the High Performance Computing(HPCC) Program. (http://www.hpcc.gov)
    1992 -NCSA shifts towards scalable metacomputing. NSF supercomputing centers are developing their own metacomputing strategies. The shift was caused by a combination of increasing price of supercomputers not matched by an increase in performance. The falling price and increasing speed of microprocessor-based parallel systems also contributed.
    1992-present
    -A race to build fast networks to support the growing number of metacomputers, to develop better technologies to manage these systems, to learn how to fully utilize, etc.
