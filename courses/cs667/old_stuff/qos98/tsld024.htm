Differentiated Services (diffserv)

  • Intended to provide scalable service discrimination in the Internet without the need for per-flow state and signaling at every hop

  • A wide range of services can be provided by - setting bits in the TOS octet at network edges - using those bits to determine how packets are treated by routers inside a network - conditioning the marked packets at network boundaries in accordance with the service needed

    Differential Services Operational Model - IETF draft
