Differentiated Services (diffserv)

    The 8-bit IPv4 TOS field and the 8-bit IPv6 Class field is redefined.
    Diffserv re-labels the byte as the DS byte ( DiffServ byte).

    IN PHB CU

    0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7

    IN : in (1) or out (0) profile
    PHB : per-hop behavior
    CU : currently unused
