HTTP/0.9 No RFC
The first version of HTTP, referred to as HTTP/0.9, was a simple protocol for raw data transfer across the Internet.
  • Single Connection
  • Remedial request transaction supported.

    • Motivations: This is was motivated simply by the need for some transmission mechanism over the internet. It was not well thought out and not tuned for performance. It was simply designed to meet the needs of the designers.
