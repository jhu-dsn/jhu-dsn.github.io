    Question : Why QoS?
  • IP offers only a best-effort model of service
  • TCP guarantees reliability and sequence
  • TCP uses reactive congestion control
  • Hence no guarantees can be made to Real-Time
    and Multimedia Traffic
  • To service such applications, the network has
    to guarantee some level of quality for the
    service it offers
