    Weighted Fair Queueing

    Tries to schedule multiple queues such that
    each of them gets a fair share of the total
    bandwidth. The weights are assigned to have
    sharing proportional to some weights.

    Token Bucket Concept

    Each Flow has tokens proportional to its
    reservation.
    Each Flow can accumulate atmost b tokens
    corresponding to its resrvation
    Each Flow gets fresh tokens as per its
    reserved bandwidth
