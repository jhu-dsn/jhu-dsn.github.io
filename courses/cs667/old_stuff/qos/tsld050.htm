The ATM Approach ...

  • In case of congestion, cells are discarded according to the Cell Loss Priority or CLP bit

  • Explicit forward congestion Indication is used to indicate that a switching element has detected the onset of congestion

  • Guarantees are only made for conforming traffic

  • In addition to the CLP bit, cell tagging may be done to indicate Delay Priorities
