Classes of Applications

  • Constant Bit Rate, or CBR

    Old voice and video codecs, Bandwidth

    allocation at ISPs, the backbone etc.

  • Variable Bit Rate, or VBR

    Bursty Media Streams like MPEG.

  • Available Bit Rate, or ABR

    Interactive Applications, like telnet, xterm where latency is important.

  • Unspecified Bit Rate, or UBR

    Non-interactive apps, like ftp and MIME mail.
