My Assessment

  • What the future direction of work would be?

  • Increasingly rather than user moving about process are being migrated.In the past programs were executed on a one single piece of fixed hardware and users interacted with it through a single fixed display device. Now the shift is for processes migrating with users.

  • This gives rise to two possibilities.

    • View mobility - requires platform and server support.
    • Platform mobility - requires only server support.
