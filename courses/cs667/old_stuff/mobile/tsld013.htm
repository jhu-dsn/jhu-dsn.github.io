Current Technologies

  • WATM

  • This is what ATM is all about.

    • In ATM the cell sizes, headers are small and data rates are high. ATM provides QOS guarantees and uniformity in handling various data types. All this seem to make ATM a good choice for handling the high demands of mobility.

  • But ATM has certain shortcomings in the wireless area.

    • ATM is designed for high bandwidth, highly reliable static links. But mobile networks provide neither high bandwidth nor reliable and secure links.
    • In the WATM the setting up of virtual paths and guaranteed QOS is severely impeded by the inherent mobility.
