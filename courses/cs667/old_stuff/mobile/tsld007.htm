Problems of mobility

  • Highly variable in performance and reliability.

    • Inside buildings where wire-line connections are available we have high speed and reliable connectivity. Outdoors we have low bandwidth wide area network, with interference, high latency. What we need are applications that adapt with this arbitrary disconnected nature of mobility, applications that are not tied to locations.

  • Inherently hazardous.

    • Portable computers are more susceptible to damage. As wireless media is used there is greater threat to data security. Designing secure and reliable wireless networks is of great importance.
