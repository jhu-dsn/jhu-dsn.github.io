Problems of mobility

  • Mobile resources are relatively resource poor.

    • We always need lightweight and small sized interfaces which can be carried around. This translates to slower processors, smaller memory size, disk capacity and screen size. Accept for foldable screens we are almost there.

  • Finite Energy Source.

    • The considerations of size and weight make battery life of mobile products very short.Conserving energy by using periodic updates, requesting and getting updates.
