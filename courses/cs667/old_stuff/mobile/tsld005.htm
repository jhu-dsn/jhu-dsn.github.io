Motivation for Mobility

    Why do we require mobility?

  • Standard

    • An executive needing immediate feedback for a deal that he is trying to close.
    • A space vehicle requiring continued access to contingency plans in case of an emergency.
    • Providing field medical staff with up to date patient records and other medical information can save lives.

  • Ad-hoc

    • Disaster recovery where infrastructure is unavailable or destroyed like in case of earthquakes, flash foods etc.
    • Students wishing to interact during a lecture .
    • Business associates wishing to share data at the airport
