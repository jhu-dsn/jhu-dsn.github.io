MPEG-1 Encoding Inter-Frame

The P and B frames can be much smaller then the I frames because of the small displacements of identifiable patterns in the picture. B frames are not used as a base for the prediction of other B or P frames, only I and P frames are used for this.

MPEG-1 allows accuracy to 1/2 or 1 Pel, and searches upto +-128 Pels for the best match.
