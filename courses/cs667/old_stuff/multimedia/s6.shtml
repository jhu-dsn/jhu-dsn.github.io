Media-Video-Encodings

  • MPEG-1
    • QCIF, Video-CD, Multimedia presentations (<1.5Mbs)

  • MPEG-2
    • CIF, HDTV,
    • Higher bitrates, numerous image enhancements,
    • support for scalable/hierarchical encoding
      • Spatial(simulcast)
      • Data(separate low and high frequency)
      • SNR
      • Temporal(real-time)

  • MPEG-4
    • Very low bit-rate(64Kb)
    • Interactive-Object oriented
    • Draft Standard Nov 1997, Int. Std. Nov 1998

  • MPEG-7
    • Meta standard for labeling and searching multimedia
    • Draft Standard in 1999/2000
    • Does NOT do Image recognition

  • H.261
    • Video conferencing at 64Kbs and up (ISDN)

  • H.263
    • Video conferencing at 14Kbs and up(POTS & Modem)

  • Intel Indeo, Cinepak
    • Proprietary, mostly video conferencing and Video-CDs
