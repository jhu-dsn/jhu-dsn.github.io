Hash Chains

  • Alice generates secret R and computes a hash chain H1(R), H2(R),… Hn(R), note that

  • Hi(R) = H(Hi-1(R))

  • Source authentication:

    • Alice sends Hn(R) initially to Bob
    • At any later i time, Alice authenticates herself by revealing Hn-i(R), Bob can verify in one step by checking
    • Hn-i(R) = H(Hn-i-1(R))
