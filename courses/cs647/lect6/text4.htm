Wireless Communication

  • 802.11b: IEEE specification for 11 Mbit/s Ethernet (CSMA/CD) running in 2.4 GHz bandwidth space

  • 802.11a: IEEE specification for 54 Mbit/s Ethernet (CSMA/CD) running in 5 GHz bandwidth space

  • Modes of operation: access-point and ad hoc
