MAC filtering

  • Hosts access the physical medium

  • via network cards.

  • Each network card is uniquely

  • identified by a 48 bit number,

  • called MAC address.

  • MAC addresses are hardwired

  • into the electronics of the network device.

  • When MAC address filtering restricts the cards that are allowed to connect to your access point.
