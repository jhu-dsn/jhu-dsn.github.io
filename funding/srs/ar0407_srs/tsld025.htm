Payment & Potential Gain

  • Protects against f Byzantine faults in each site for the price of having 3f+1 replicas in every site.
  • Box numbers / a total site compromise.
  • Read queries are limited to the local site.
  • On a network with diameter of 50 ms.
    • It takes at least 300 milliseconds to complete 3 wide area round trips used by peer Byzantine replication methods.
    • FT Replication engine was shown to be achieve 5 times the performance of 2PC.
  • Goal
    • > factor of 3 compared with a peer system.
