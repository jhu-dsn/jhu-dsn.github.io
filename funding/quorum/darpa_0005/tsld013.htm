Extension: Real Time

  • Some jobs have a “real time constraint” or maximum slowdown.

  • The system makes a Quality of Service guarantee:

    • The job will have slowdown less than the maximum allowed slowdown.

  • Admission control:

    • The system can reject jobs as necessary to meet guarantees.
    • Can use a benefit function to decide which jobs to reject.
