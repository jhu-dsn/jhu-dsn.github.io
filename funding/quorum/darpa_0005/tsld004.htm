The Cost-Benefit Framework

  • A new online assignment policy based on economic principles, competitive analysis.

  • Guarantees near-optimal global lower-bound performance.

  • Converts usage of heterogeneous resources (CPU, memory, IO) into a single, homogeneous cost using a specific cost function. (comparing apples and oranges).

  • The main concept is system independent.
