Extension: Incomplete resource requirements a-priori knowledge

  • In the real world, tasks rarely know their resource requirements in advance.

  • Less of an issue if migration is possible, more problematic if not.

  • Our Idea: Approximate cost by the differential of the cost function. (The cost for a task imposing an infinitesimal consumption on each resource.)

  • Results: PVM slowdown 14.08, Differential-PVM 10.7, EPVM with full knowledge 9.6.

  • Implication: Although the knowledge is incomplete, managed to capture 75% of the improvement.
