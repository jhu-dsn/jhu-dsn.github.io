Replication

  • Why Replication?

    • Replication can mask network problems.
      • Active defense against denial of service attacks.

  • Questions:

    • How many replicas do we need? (dynamic).
    • Where do we place them? (dynamic).

  • Goal:

    • Efficient, flexible replication tools.
    • Allow many tradeoffs in performance and fault-tolerance.
    • Calls for the Cost-Benefit Framework.
