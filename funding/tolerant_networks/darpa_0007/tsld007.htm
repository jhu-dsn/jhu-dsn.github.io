The Cost-Benefit Framework

  • An online decision-making concept based on economic principles, competitive analysis.

  • Converts usage of heterogeneous resources (CPU, memory, bandwidth, disk space) into a single, homogeneous cost (comparing apples and oranges).

  • Admission control based on benefit.

  • Often guarantees near-optimal global lower-bound performance.

  • The main concept is system independent.
