Adversary Models

  • Temporal correlation

    • Predictable versus non predictable adversary: is past correlated with future?

  • Stability

    • Global stability, path stability, no stability: is the network (or path) stable for a large enough period of time compared with round trip delay?

  • Selectivity

    • Selective versus uniform disruption: is there discrimination between types of traffic? (blocking only specific data packets, e.g. of specific source or type).
