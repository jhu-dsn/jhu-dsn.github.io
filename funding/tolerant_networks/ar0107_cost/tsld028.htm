Replication

  • Replication can mask network problems.

    • Example: A tool against denial of service.

  • Questions:

    • How many replicas do we need? (dynamic).
    • Where do we place them? (dynamic).
    • Which replication method to use? (primary backup, anti-entropy, peer (active) replication).

  • Tradeoffs – more replicas means:

    • Lower communication cost for access, lower connectivity requirements.
    • Higher costs for updates, higher space requirements.
    • Calls for the Cost-Benefit Framework.
