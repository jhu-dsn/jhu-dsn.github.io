Technical Approach

  • Modeling:

    • Classification of adversary models.

  • Algorithms:

    • Strong adversaries can be defeated on the network level and on the application level.

  • Network level resiliency:

    • Near-optimal routing and dissemination.

  • Information access resiliency:

    • Replication with graceful degradation of performance and semantics as network hostility increases.

  • Integrating resource management with reliability:

    • A cost benefit / economics framework.
