Technical Objective

  • Providing a high performance, robust and secure group communication service for wide area and local area networks: Secure Spread.

  • Secure Spread should scale to tens of coalition parties with hundreds of servers, supporting thousands of users.

  • Collaboratively developing flexible and provably reliable and secure protocols. Some unique aspects:

    • Multiple autonomous control domains.
    • Dynamic trust.
